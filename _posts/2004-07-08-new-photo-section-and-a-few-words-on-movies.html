Victor (Victor Rasuk) is a Latino kid who fancies himself as a bit of a lothario around the ghetto. So, he finds himself a hot new girlfriend (Judy Marte) to shut everyone up. Yea... the movie wasn't really about that. Check IMDB for reviews.
