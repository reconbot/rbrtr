SubEthaEdit brings in network sharing of a document and other nifty things you can read about everything on your own. I bring this all up because I added the extension tpl to the smarty-html mode. You can download it here. I did it because I was sick of all my .tpl files not automaticly highlighting. (.tpl is smarty's default file extension for template files.) And I put it here to show off SubEthaEdit and it's html export and because at least one person I know will find it useful. I learned of one extra feature from SubEthaEdit today called block editing.
